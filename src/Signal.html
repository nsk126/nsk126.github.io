Simple Signal Generator.

Signals take the form of A*Sin(2*Π*f*t)

Where A is the Amputute or height of the signal. f is the Frequency of the signal. And t is the time resolution of the signal


The above graph is a simple demo where a signal with parameters
A = 1, f = 4 and t = 0.0001
The y-axis is the calculated value of A*Sin(2*Π*f*t) and the x-axis is the calculated value of 2*Π*f*t. This implies, that the signal generated would have an absolute maximum value of 1, repeat 4 times in the time period of interest, with a resolution of 0.0001.
